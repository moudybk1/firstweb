Moudy, the story is begin

My name is Yoga Bayu, also known as moudy because that is my nickname on Mobile Legends game. I am someone with a great curiosity and always interested in learning new things.

Started as a noob trader..

The good old days, this began when I felt like "oh i was become a man" but i still couldn't do anything. Then, I thought about selling my game account and using it to start crypto trading on a cryptocurrency exchange (cex), and one thing I regret at that time is that I sold BNB for $40.

Become an airdrop hunter

Initially, I didn't understand how to earning from cryptocurrency other than trading, but a friend taught me how to make money by becoming an airdrop hunter. From this, I learned a lot—how these projects operate, how to get significant engagement, and, of course, how we make money from airdrops. It turns out the crypto world is very vast.

Work as a Discord Moderator on some projects

When I started to understand the workflow in the web3 world, I wanted to try registering as a moderator for various projects on Solana, until eventually, I became a moderator for Aurory Project, Cykura Finance, and Fluidity Money. Also, I am currently still the leader of the Offchain Global Chapter Yogyakarta, where I once organized gathering events with other web3 enthusiasts.

FTX Collapse

I still remember in November 2022, all the money I had earned couldn't be withdrawn from FTX. On that day, I felt shattered and lost. Until eventually, it made me realize that I had to complete my studies in economics. There, I learned about macroeconomics, microeconomics, and econometrics.

Learning new thing

After completing my academic obligations, I became interested in learning something new—programming. Although I didn't have a basic knowledge of it, my desire to learn was strong. Eventually, I managed to create this amateur website through self-learning. Even though the implementation is not perfect, I feel happy because I have achieved something like this.
